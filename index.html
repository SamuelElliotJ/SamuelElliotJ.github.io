Hi!

My name is Samuel Elliot Johnson, I am a passionate Technical Designer based in the UK who loves to play and break down games to figure out how they work.

Background

I started to pursue a career in developing video games in 2018, I went to college to learn games design and programming, and then continued that journey in University. During this time I developed a passion for Unreal Engine and Unity as well as Technical Design. The most enjoyable part of Technical Design and Programming is problem solving, breaking down mechanics and rebuilding them is a creative outlet for me and a way to express myself.


Now I am a Gameplay Design and Production student at Staffordshire University (soon to be graduate) focusing on Technical Design, based in Stoke-on-Trent, UK. When I am not working on projects for University you might find me working on a passion project, games jam, or exercising. I also love playing a large variety of games, "The Legend of Heroes" series being my current favourite.

Featured Project

"Get Reel is a relaxing fish catching game for krilling time!"


This project was made in 8 weeks as part of my third year senior collaborative module. For this project i acted as the project lead and since the tech team was relatively small with around 5 people I also acted as tech lead which is where most of my work went to.
