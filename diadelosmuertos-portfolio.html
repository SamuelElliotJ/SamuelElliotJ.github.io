Dia De Los Muertos

I worked on this project for a games jam hosted by Staffordshire university. We had 1 week to create a short game as a group of 4.

Dia De Los Muertos is a game inspired by the mexican day of the dead. The player can swap between the realm of the dead and the realm of the living revealing different blocks in order to get through the city as fast as possible.

My role:

-Sole programmer

I was the sole programmer for this project and i programmed everything using visual script. The videos below showcase some of what i did as well as the final project.
